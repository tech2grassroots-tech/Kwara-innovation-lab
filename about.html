Our Vision

A Digital Africa, Built from the Ground Up

We envision an Africa where every young person regardless of geography, background, or economic status has access to world-class technology education and the opportunity to build a meaningful career in tech.

Kwara Innovation Lab is the flagship implementation of that vision. We believe that grassroots communities are not obstacles to innovation they are its greatest untapped source.

lightbulb
Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide by equipping individuals, schools, educators, startups, and organizations with practical digital skills, innovative solutions, and technology-driven opportunities that foster sustainable growth and global competitiveness.

Our Goal

Train 10,000 grassroots tech talents by 2028, establishing hubs in every Nigerian state and expanding across 5 African countries.

Our Network

Expanding Across Nigeria & Africa

What started as a single lab in Ilorin is now a growing constellation of connected innovation hubs, each one bringing the same quality training to a new community.

Central Command

Ilorin Hub

Our flagship facility at the Kwara State Library Complex. Home base for curriculum development, enterprise training, and innovation incubation.

location_on Kwara State Library Complex, Ilorin
Active Hub

Lagos Hub

Bridging Kwara talent to Lagos' thriving tech ecosystem. Focused on enterprise placements and startup incubation for graduates.

location_on Lagos State, Nigeria
Active Hub

Osun Hub

Serving Oshogbo and surrounding communities with hands-on bootcamps and digital skills training for youth and young professionals.
